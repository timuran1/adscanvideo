From Video to Marketing Copy: A Step-by-Step Guide

Marketing · July 2026 · 4 min read

You recorded a video. Or you found a competitor's video that's performing well. Now what do you do with it?

Most people watch it, nod, and move on. But that video contains dozens of marketing assets — headlines, hooks, social captions, LinkedIn posts — just waiting to be extracted.

Why Video Is a Goldmine for Copy

A 60-second video typically contains:

  • 3-5 distinct hook ideas (from the opening seconds)
  • 5-10 compelling phrases or word choices
  • A clear problem-solution structure
  • Emotional triggers (visual + audio)
  • A call to action you can adapt

How to Extract Copy from Any Video

Step 1: Analyze the video

Drop it into AdScanVideo in "Marketing copy" mode. The AI will extract the hook, structure, key phrases, and emotional triggers automatically.

Step 2: Repurpose into formats

From one analysis, you can generate:

  • 3 social captions — short, punchy, platform-optimized
  • 3 LinkedIn posts — professional, insight-driven
  • 5 blog headlines — different angles for the same topic
  • 3 hook ideas — for promoting the content elsewhere

Step 3: Adapt for your audience

Swap the specific product references with your own. Keep the structure, pacing, and emotional arc that made the original effective.

💡 Pro tip: The best-performing social posts are often just a great video insight, reformatted. Don't create from scratch — find, analyze, adapt.
