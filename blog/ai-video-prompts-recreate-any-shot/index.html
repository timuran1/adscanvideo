AI Video Prompts: How to Recreate Any Shot from a Reference

AI Video · July 2026 · 4 min read

Writing good AI video prompts from scratch is hard. Describing a shot in a way that Seedance, Kling, Sora, or Veo understands takes practice — and even then, results are unpredictable.

But there's a shortcut: let a real video write the prompts for you.

What Makes a Good AI Video Prompt?

AI video models need specific information to produce consistent results. A strong prompt includes:

  • Subject — what or who is in frame
  • Action — what's happening (movement, expression, interaction)
  • Environment — setting, lighting, time of day
  • Camera — angle, movement, lens (wide, close-up, tracking)
  • Mood — tone, color palette, atmosphere
  • Aspect ratio — 16:9, 9:16, 1:1

Real Example: From Reference to Prompt

Reference shot (from a car ad):

Aerial drone shot, luxury sedan driving along a coastal highway at golden hour. Sun reflects off the ocean. Smooth, cinematic. 5 seconds.

AI prompt (Seedance format):

Aerial drone shot tracking a black luxury sedan driving along a coastal highway at golden hour, warm sunlight reflecting off the ocean surface, smooth camera movement, cinematic composition, shallow depth of field, 16:9, warm color palette, premium atmosphere, slow pace.
